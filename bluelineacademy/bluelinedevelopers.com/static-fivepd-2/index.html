FAQ

hOW DO I USE THE RADIO?

Radio Channels:

KEY: F5
COMMAND: /RADIO
PUSH TO TALK: LEFT ALT

  • BCSO: Channel #3
  • LSPD: Channel #4
  • SAHP: Channel #5
  • LSFD: Channel #6
  • DOT: Channel #7
  • LSCSO: Channel #9

Radio Etiquette: Effective communication is key for teamwork and coordination. Follow these guidelines:

  • Channel Selection: Use channel 3 if fewer than 12 members are online; otherwise, refer to the assigned channels for each department.
  • Clarity: Clearly communicate your location, status, and any assistance needed.
  • Conciseness: Keep radio chatter brief and relevant to avoid cluttering the channel.
  • Etiquette: Adhere to professional standards and respect other users on the radio.
How do I get my uniform?

Start by making an MPed to do so, press M to open the vMenu. Go to player option and create a new character. Once you have completed making the character you can then use the command /eup to access the EUP menu which has pre-set Uniforms for all departments.

How do I spawn a car?

Spawn vehicles by using /vs . If it says the vehicle is restricted you are likely not a high enough rank to be driving it. Please view the ranking up channel in discord to learn more!

How do I change the livery on the vehicle?

Using the vMenu go to Vehicle Options, then select Liveries

How do I go on duty and get my loadout?

Press F11 to access the FivePD menu, here you can go on duty and accuire a loadout.

Why am I not receiving callouts?

Please make sure you on duty, you must also join a department within the CAD to receive callouts.
